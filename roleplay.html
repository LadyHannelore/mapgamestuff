RP Grading

Flesh out your nation's story while getting paid. There will be a cap of 10 RP posts bi-weekly per player. In which, within the 2 week period they will be graded and silver given out as if it was a Rank 2 submission.

Events

Events are massive roleplay interactions that have the potential to impact the whole world. These come in two types:

Player Events

Created when multiple players begin to encroach upon each other in a questline. These are hosted by one player who sets the ground rules for the event and designs it. Rewards are set by moderators.

Server Events

Spawned in by a moderator and cover such instances as natural disasters, real life holidays and global economic crashes or booms. These events are designed by a moderator without player input.

To join an event you must read the roleplay post that began it which will outline the specific requirements for participation.

Quests

Quests are exclusive storylines for the best roleplayers that bring the world to life. They function as narratives split into several objectives that you complete through roleplay. At the end of a quest you unlock plenty of material rewards such as bonus resources, elite units and silver.

Examples of possible Quests include:

  • Finding ancient relics
  • Leading pilgrimages
  • Expeditions to discover new continents
  • Coronations
  • Rebellions
  • Miracles
  • Catastrophes

There is no way for a player to request a quest but you may increase your chances of getting one by posting lots of quality roleplay with a recurring through line to catch the eye of a moderator who then may create one for you.
