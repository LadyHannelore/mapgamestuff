Warfare

The Basics

War is fought between 2 or more nations with a valid Justification. Wars are fought with Brigades, which act as individual units. Generals allow you to combine brigades into an Army.

  • Action Cycle: Wars run on a 3-day action cycle (Tue-Thu, Fri-Sun) that determines allowed actions (organization, movement, combat).
  • Brigade Cap: Starts at 2 and increases with cities (T1: +1, T2: +3, T3: +5).
  • War College: Grants General benefits and levels up with General retirements and won wars.
  • Peace Treaties: End wars and must follow justification stipulations. Carry a 2-week non-aggression pact.
  • War Room: A private channel for all your warfare decisions.

War Justifications

Every offensive war requires a valid justification. Each has specific requirements, victory/defeat conditions, and peace terms.

When taking tiles in a peace deal, city tiles have a higher cost:

  • Tier 1 City: Costs 2 tiles.
  • Tier 2 City: Costs 3 tiles.
  • Tier 3 City: Costs 4 tiles.

Generals

Generals lead your armies, providing powerful bonuses and abilities. They are recruited at the War College and gain experience from battles.

Generals have a chance to gain a new trait upon leveling up. These traits provide unique advantages in combat.

Trait Categories:

  • Offensive: Increase damage or effectiveness in battle.
  • Defensive: Enhance survivability and reduce damage taken.
  • Support: Provide bonuses to allied brigades or generals.
  • Logistical: Improve movement, supply, and strategic options.

Example Traits:

  • Strategist: +1 to all tactical rolls.
  • Shield Bearer: +2 Defense to self and adjacent brigades.
  • Warrior's Spirit: +1 Skirmish and +1 Pitch.
  • Logistics Expert: +1 Movement and -1 supply cost.

War College

The War College is essential for your military operations. It allows you to recruit and manage your generals, and it levels up as your nation gains military experience.

Retiring a general at level 10 provides a significant experience boost to the War College.

War College Benefits

  • Increased General Cap: Allows you to recruit more generals.
  • New Brigade Enhancements: Unlocks powerful upgrades for your brigades.
  • Improved General Traits: Higher chance of gaining beneficial traits.
