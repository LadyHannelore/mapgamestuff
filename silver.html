🏭 Resource Generation

Resources are generated through various means including territory control, city production, trade, and worldbuilding submissions.

🗺️ Territory Production

Different terrain types and resource deposits on your territory generate specific resources each cycle.

🏙️ City Production

Cities generate resources based on their tier and location. Higher tier cities produce more resources.

🤝 Trade Networks

Establish trade relationships with other players to exchange resources. Bullion is used to pay trading costs.

📝 Worldbuilding

Submit worldbuilding content to earn resources based on quality and relevance to your nation's development.

💡 Strategic Resource Management

🎯 Prioritization

  • • Focus on resources needed for your immediate goals
  • • Balance expansion resources (Food/Fuel) with military resources (Metal)
  • • Secure Luxuries early for diplomatic flexibility
  • • Maintain Bullion reserves for trading opportunities

🔄 Exchange Opportunities

  • • Technocratic governments can exchange 3:1 of same resource
  • • Trade with other players for resources you need
  • • Consider government modifiers that boost specific resources
  • • Plan resource allocation around terrain expansion costs
