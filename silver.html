Mondays will no longer permit silver submissions or RP, as that is now the day that grading will occur. This allows graders to get caught up and everyone to get their silver.

Monthly Tourneys

Tournaments are monthly optional activities for players to compete in for various prizes. There are four categories:

I: Essay-writing

A prompt will be agreed upon by the contestants, which must be submitted as an essay with a word count between 500 and 1000 words. A sensibly flexible deadline will be in place and each essay will be judged by its quality first and foremost.

II: Debates

A question will be agreed upon by the contestants, and each must pick a side and write a main argument for that side with a hard word cap of 150 words. Then, they must submit a counter to each other's main arguments consisting of no more than 100 words.

III: Arts

A specific prompt and format relating to a historical topic will be agreed upon by the two sides â€” such as a map, a comic strip, a drawing, a poster, etc. â€” and submitted within a reasonable deadline to be judged by the relevant moderator.

IV: Memes

A prompt or a topic will be decided upon by the two sides in the bracket and memed, to be judged before the entire server via voting through reactions.

*If no topic can be decided upon within a reasonable time, the judge is free to pick as they please.
