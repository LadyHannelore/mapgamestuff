ğŸ“ Worldbuilding Submissions

Each week, you may create 3 (by default) worldbuilding submissions, which can be posted /here/, alongside indicating 1 resource of your choice. Once approved, the resource will be added to your national stockpile.

Worldbuilding submissions can be essays about real or alternate history, RP, graphical designs or drawings, maps, audio pieces such as music, speeches, or hymns, each of which must relate to your nation or territory.

Submissions of very low quality or effort may be denied, and text submissions should be at least 200 words. Exceptional work may be nominated into the Hall of Fame, where they will be posted prominently for everyone to see and receive additional resource rewards.

Weekly Deadline: The cutoff for Worldbuilding Submissions every week is Friday night. DO NOT post them on Saturday or Sunday.

âš ï¸ Resource Debt

Though you generally cannot use resources you do not have stockpiled, you may end up negative on resources at the start of a turn if your upkeep is greater than your resource gain. If this occurs, you will start to accrue penalties based on what resource is and how delinquent you are. The effects are cumulative, so every previous penalty also occurs upon the beginning of the week.

Going Bankrupt in more than a single resource in the same week causes a revolution, giving you a randomized government. This breaks any treaties, truces, etc that were held by the previous government.
