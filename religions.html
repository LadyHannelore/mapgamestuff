üïäÔ∏è Religion System

Everyone has a religion, which they can choose upon spawning. Each has a set of abilities. There are several base religions, which then can "schism" based on player count.

‚õ™ Schism & Player Count

Every time this happens the new religions will keep the old bonuses and also get a new bonus. This starts with five people, and doubles every subsequent schism. Certain religions will be 'capstone' religions, like Catholicism. These religions don't break on a Schism but still get a new bonus.

Regardless of size a player always counts as at least one player for a religion. However, for every 20 tiles they own over the initial 20, they count as an additional player for the religion. For example, a player with 100 tiles is five players for Schism calculation. Co-op players may also count towards a Schism player count, always as one player.

üîÑ Conversion

Willing Conversion takes place when a player adopts the Theocratic government modifier, or pays a cost to convert to a visiting diplomat's religion. This cost is 1 Bullion for every 3 City Weight you have, rounded up.

Forced Conversion occurs when you lose a Holy War, or when certain religions convert you forcibly through diplomats. Only the Theocratic government modifier grants Holy War justification.
