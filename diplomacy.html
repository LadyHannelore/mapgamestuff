Diplomacy

Alliances

Alliances are formal agreements between two or more nations. They can be defensive, offensive, or based on shared culture or religion.

  • Defensive Pacts: Nations agree to defend each other if one is attacked.
  • Offensive Pacts: Nations agree to join each other in offensive wars.
  • Non-Aggression Pacts (NAPs): Nations agree not to attack each other for a set period of time.
  • Trade Pacts: Nations agree to favorable trade terms.

Wars

Wars are declared through a formal process. A Casus Belli (reason for war) is required.

  • Declaration of War: Must be announced publicly with a clear Casus Belli.
  • War Goals: The objectives of the war must be stated.
  • Peace Treaties: Wars end with a peace treaty, negotiated between the warring parties.

Treaties

Treaties are formal agreements that can end wars, establish trade relations, or define borders.

All treaties must be made public.
