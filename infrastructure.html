Infrastructure

Resources

  • Each province produces 1 resource.
  • Each province can have a total of 5 infrastructure points.
  • These can be used on:
    • Forts (max 5)
    • Production buildings (max 5)
  • Production buildings increase the amount of resources a province produces:
    • Tier 1: 1 building = +1 resource (cost 1)
    • Tier 2: 2 buildings = +2 resources (cost 2)
    • Tier 3: 3 buildings = +3 resources (cost 3)
    • Tier 4: 4 buildings = +4 resources (cost 4)
    • Tier 5: 5 buildings = +5 resources (cost 5)

Cities

  • Cities are hubs of trade and commerce.
  • They do not produce resources, but instead generate silver.
  • Cities can have a total of 10 infrastructure points.
  • These can be used on:
    • Forts (max 5)
    • Trade buildings (max 10)
  • Trade buildings increase the amount of silver a city produces:
    • Tier 1: 1 building = +1 silver (cost 1)
    • Tier 2: 2 buildings = +2 silver (cost 2)
    • Tier 3: 3 buildings = +3 silver (cost 3)
    • Tier 4: 4 buildings = +4 silver (cost 4)
    • Tier 5: 5 buildings = +5 silver (cost 5)
    • Tier 6: 6 buildings = +6 silver (cost 6)
    • Tier 7: 7 buildings = +7 silver (cost 7)
    • Tier 8: 8 buildings = +8 silver (cost 8)
    • Tier 9: 9 buildings = +9 silver (cost 9)
    • Tier 10: 10 buildings = +10 silver (cost 10)

Trading

  • Players can trade resources and silver with each other.
  • Trade is done through trade routes, which can be established between cities.
  • The amount of trade that can be done is limited by the number of trade buildings in a city.
