🎮 What is Hegemony?

Hegemony is a Discord-based grand strategy role-playing game that focuses on strategic resource management and warfare, nation/world building, competitive and cooperative community events, and roleplay. We have a consistent and close-knit community that we encourage others to become a part of.

🗺️ Map & Expansions: Strategic territorial expansion with terrain-based costs and natural border considerations.
📦 Resource Management: Six essential resources drive the entire economy: Bullion, Food, Fuel, Luxuries, Material, and Metal.
🏛️ Government Systems: Choose from Democracy, Oligarchy, or Autocracy with specialized modifiers that shape your playstyle.
🏰 Warfare: Engage in strategic combat with other nations, using a variety of units, generals, and tactics.
⚡ Battle Simulator: Test your army compositions and strategies in a mock battle environment before committing to a real war.
🙏 Religions: Seven distinct religions each offering unique bonuses and roleplay opportunities.
🏗️ Infrastructure: Build and upgrade cities, manage terrain effects, and develop your nation's infrastructure.
🎭 Roleplay: Engage in immersive worldbuilding and roleplay events that shape your nation's story.
🤝 Diplomacy: Form alliances, forge treaties, and engage in complex diplomatic relations.
⛵ Naval Operations: Build and command naval forces for overseas expansion and maritime warfare.

🌟 Starting Your Journey

Begin with 20 tiles and 3 cities. Expand strategically within terrain limitations, manage six essential resources, and shape your nation through government choices and diplomatic relationships.

Step-by-step instructions for joining the game

✅ Before You Spawn

  • • Check the latest /main map/ for available territory
  • • Familiarize yourself with the /Player Sheet/
  • • Choose your government type and modifiers
  • • Select a religion that fits your playstyle
  • • Plan your 20 starting tiles and 3 city locations
  • • Prepare your spawn request post

🎯 Your First Week

  • • Submit up to 3 worldbuilding submissions for resources
  • • Plan your first expansion (remember the 4-tile city range!)
  • • Consider early diplomatic contacts
  • • Start developing your nation's roleplay and culture
  • • Join community discussions and events
  • • Ask questions in the appropriate channels

📜 Rules

⚠️ Important: Please read all rules carefully before participating!

  1. 🔒 Discord Guidelines: Follow Discord TOS and Community Guidelines at all times.
  2. 🤖 AI Prohibition: The use of AI is PROHIBITED for anything related to the mechanical playing of the game, including earning resources, winning tournaments, and establishing treaties.
  3. 📚 Tutorial Requirement: All players must read the tutorials before asking questions related to mechanics or gameplay.
  4. 👑 Administrator Authority: Administrators ALWAYS have the final say in anything on this server. Personal conflicts between players and moderators, disagreement on rulings, or anything else that needs an administrator should be brought to their attention swiftly, and their arbitration should be respected.
  5. 🎭 Roleplay Focus: Making in-game decisions based on out-of-game events and opinions, while not outright banned, is heavily frowned upon. This is ultimately a game based on Roleplay, and we expect a concerted effort to avoid metagaming.
  6. 🚫 No Promotion: No promotion of any other servers is to be permitted without Administrator approval.
  7. ⚔️ Warfare Respect: Warfare is a key part of this game and the failure to act with respect and courtesy toward your enemies will not be tolerated.
  8. 🔐 War Room Privacy: Sharing any visual evidence of your War Room is grounds for an immediate Timeout with no exceptions. DO NOT SHARE PICTURES OF YOUR WAR ROOM. You may discuss the contents with anyone, so long as there is no visual evidence shared between you.
